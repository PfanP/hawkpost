HAWKPOST

when a pigeon is not enough!

If receiving confidential information in non-safe places makes you crazy, Hawkpost is for you!
No email, no tricky programs, just a link to share information safely.

How does it work?

1

Register your PGP key.

2

Send the link to share information - Wait for your friend write/upload the information.

3

Receive the information safely by our hawk in your e-mail.

FAQ

Why should I use this?

This is the easiest way to receive sensible information. No one needs to install anything, you share your link, the person who receive it just need to submit the information and our hawk will send it for you.

Why is this safe?

Hawkpost uses end-to-end encryption, which means that the submitted information will be only visible for who create the link and we will not save it.

What type of information can be submitted?

Any text information can be submitted and only be visible for the person who create the link.
