Hawpost. When a pigeon is not enough

If receiving confidential information in non-safe places makes you crazy, Hawkpost is for you!
No email, no tricky programs, just a link to share information safely.

PGP encripted information delivered right to your inbox.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Register your PGP key.

Send the link to share information - Wait for your friend write/upload the information.

Our hawk will deliver the encrypted message to your e-mail.

FAQ

How does it work behind the curtain?

The concept is simple, you generate an unique link on Hawkpost, then you share it with someone that needs to send you some secret information. When that person visits the link and submits the sensitive data, it is encrypted in their browser with your PGP public key and then forwarded to your email address. No data is stored by us.

Why should I use this instead of blha?

This is the easiest way to securely receive sensible information from people who don't know how to use PGP. They don't even need to install anything: you just share your link and the person who receives it just needs to submit the information. Our hawk will send it back to you.

Why is this safe?

Hawkpost provides end-to-end encryption using OpenPGP.js library, which means that the submitted information will be only visible to whom created the link (the owner of the key) and we will not save it.

Is my link permanently available?

No, at the momment you can create as many unique links as you want, one for each ocasion. All of them should have an expiration date, however other configuration options will be added soon, so you can adapt the functionality to your own use cases.

What type of information can be submitted?

Any text information can be submitted and only be visible for the person who created the link.
