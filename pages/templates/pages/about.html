Hawkpost

A lot of years ago pigeons were used to send private messages to people. But to send private and confidential information a pigeon is not enough. We upgraded the pigeon for a hawk, it's stronger, faster and will protect better all your messages.

We know that sometimes it's difficult to explain how to encrypt things or how to install that program that will protect confidential information. That's why we created "Hawkpost", a place where someone can easily submit and send sensible information for you in a safe way.

This project was made at Whitesmith's Hackathon during our company retreat. The aim of this hackathon was to launch a side-project in 24h.
